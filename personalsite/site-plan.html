Menu

Indulge in our American Cheese Burger: juicy beef patty, melted American cheese, soft toasted bun. Classic flavor, pure satisfaction.

Introducing our refreshing Boba Milk Tea (No Tea): creamy milk infused with sweet tapioca pearls. Sip, chew, and savor the delightful fusion of flavors and textures. A creamy delight awaits with every sip!

Dive into deliciousness with our Chips & Dip: crispy, golden chips paired with creamy, flavorful dip. Perfect for sharing or savoring solo. Dip into a world of flavor!

Treat yourself to a scoop of happiness with our creamy, indulgent ice cream. Made with the finest ingredients, each spoonful is a burst of sweet perfection. Dive into a world of cool delight!

About Us

Discover Boba Burger where flavors collide and memories are made. Located in the heart of Rexburg, our cozy spot offers a culinary adventure unlike any other. From our diverse menu to our welcoming atmosphere, we're here to elevate your dining experience. Join us for a meal and let us show you why Boba Burger is the talk of the town.

At Boba Burger, expansion is on the horizon. We're gearing up to bring our culinary experience to more neighborhoods with new locations in the pipeline. Stay tuned for updates as we grow and continue to share our passion for food with even more communities.

At Boba Burger, community matters. We source locally, support local initiatives, and actively engage in neighborhood events. From partnering with nearby farms to hosting fundraisers, we're committed to making a positive impact right where we are.

At Boba Burger, customer service is paramount. From the moment you step through our doors, expect nothing but exceptional service and attention to detail. Our friendly staff is here to cater to your every need, ensuring a memorable dining experience from start to finish. Come join us and let us exceed your expectations at Boba Burger.
